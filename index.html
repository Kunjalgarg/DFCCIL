About Us

The Dedicated Freight Corridor Corporation of India Limited (DFCCIL), established in 2006 by the Government of India, is responsible for developing and managing dedicated freight corridors to improve the speed and efficiency of freight transportation. It operates major corridors like the Western and Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridors to ease congestion and boost logistics. DFCCIL incorporates advanced technologies such as electrification and automation, focusing on sustainability with eco-friendly practices like electric traction. Its efforts contribute to industrial growth, reduce logistics costs, and create job opportunities, while collaborating with international organizations like the World Bank and JICA for funding and expertise.

Our Achievements

  • Development of Dedicated Freight Corridors (WDFC & EDFC): Successfully executed major freight corridors to ease congestion and improve efficiency.
  • Increased Freight Capacity: Enhanced the transportation capacity of Indian railways, benefiting industries like manufacturing and agriculture.
  • Technological Advancements: Adoption of advanced signaling systems, automation, and electrification for improved safety and efficiency.
